ABOUT

Agrobiz is an initiative that allows you to buy your agricultural products directly from our farmers at affordable prices. It was founded in the year 2022 by the Famous Five Friends. It was established for the individuals in the society to acquire agricultural products at a reasonable and affordable price.

As we all know, Nigeria is facing a lot of problems regarding agricultural aspect.After much thinking the Five Famous Friends decided to provide a solution to eradicate one of the problems in the society. It is a website built to create awareness to individual who are yet to know about the usefulness of agricultural products.
